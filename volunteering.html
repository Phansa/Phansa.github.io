Volunteering

I helped start a volunteering group in December 2012 to give back to the Indian community. It was a lot of work to start the youth group with my friends but I would say it was a very fun experience. As part of this group, we would go to various local nursing homes and perform a music/dance show to entertain the senior citizens there. Today it's still active and new people have joined the group so I would say that it was successful. Our website can be found here . I was invited to give a speech at the Ekal Vidyalaya national conference about how other people could start similiar youth groups and give back to their communities.
